# Content Writing Best Practices

## Hook Formula
1. **Question hook:** "Pernah tak...?"
2. **Shock hook:** "Ramai tak tahu..."
3. **Story hook:** "3 tahun lepas, aku..."
4. **Number hook:** "5 sebab kenapa..."

## Post Structure
1. **HOOK** (line 1 — stop the scroll)
2. **STORY/VALUE** (body — deliver the goods)
3. **CTA** (last line — tell them what to do)
4. **HASHTAGS** (10-15, mix popular + niche)

## Platform-Specific
- **Instagram:** Visual-first, carousel performs best
- **Facebook:** Longer captions OK, engagement hooks work
- **TikTok:** Short, punchy, trend-aware
- **Threads:** Conversational, thread-style
